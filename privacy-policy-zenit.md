# Privacy Policy for Zenith: Face Yoga

**Effective Date:** June 25, 2025
**Last Updated:** June 25, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Zenith: Face Yoga ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not download, register with, or use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide
- **Profile Information:** Name, age, gender, display name
- **Account Information:** Email address (for registered users)
- **Wellness Data:** Focus areas (facial regions), personal goals, time commitment preferences, skin type, routine timing preferences, appearance satisfaction ratings
- **Progress Photos:** Before/after photos you choose to take and store (stored locally on your device)
- **Communication:** Messages you send through our AI chat feature (ZenithAI)

### Information Automatically Collected
- **Usage Data:** App features used, time spent in app, exercise completion rates, progress tracking
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, app version, unique device identifiers
- **Performance Data:** App performance metrics, crash reports, memory usage (anonymized)
- **Analytics Data:** Screen navigation patterns, feature usage statistics (anonymized)

### Information from Third Parties
- **Subscription Data:** Purchase information and subscription status from RevenueCat and app stores
- **AI Processing:** Conversation context processed by Google Gemini AI for personalized responses

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

### Primary Functions
- Provide personalized face yoga training programs and recommendations
- Track your progress and maintain exercise streaks
- Generate custom 12-week wellness plans using AI technology
- Deliver daily reminders and notifications based on your preferences
- Enable photo progress tracking with before/after comparisons

### Service Improvement
- Analyze app usage to improve features and user experience
- Develop new exercises and content based on user preferences
- Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
- Debug and fix technical issues to ensure app stability

### AI-Powered Features
- Process conversations with ZenithAI to provide personalized advice
- Analyze user goals and preferences to customize workout recommendations
- Generate contextual responses based on your profile and chat history

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage (On Your Device)
- Progress photos are stored locally on your device
- User preferences and settings
- Chat conversation history
- Exercise progress and completion data
- Cached content for offline functionality

### Cloud Storage (Supabase)
- User profiles and account information
- Exercise progress and completion tracking
- 12-week personalized training plans
- Anonymized usage analytics

### Security Measures
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Sensitive information stored using secure storage methods
- Database access protected with Row Level Security (RLS)
- API keys and sensitive configuration data never stored in the app
- Regular security audits and updates

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Third-Party Service Providers
We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our App:

- **Supabase:** Database and authentication services (data stored securely with encryption)
- **Google Gemini AI:** AI conversation processing (temporary context only, not permanently stored)
- **RevenueCat:** Subscription management and analytics (purchase data only)
- **Expo:** App distribution, updates, and crash reporting (anonymized data)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in good faith belief that such action is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations or court orders
- Protect and defend our rights or property
- Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the App
- Protect personal safety of users or the public

### Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

## Your Privacy Rights and Choices

### Account Management
- **Access and Update:** You can access and update your profile information within the App
- **Account Deletion:** You can delete your account, which will remove your cloud data
- **Anonymous Usage:** You can use most app features without creating an account

### Data Control
- **Photo Management:** Progress photos are stored locally; you control when to take, view, or delete them
- **Chat History:** You can delete individual chat conversations with ZenithAI
- **Notifications:** You can disable or customize notification preferences
- **Cache Clearing:** You can clear cached content and temporary data

#### EU/UK Rights (GDPR)
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectify inaccurate data
- Right to erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

#### California Rights (CCPA)
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and include appropriate safeguards to protect your information.

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

- **Account Information:** Retained while your account is active
